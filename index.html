About Dr. Huma Hameed

Dr. Huma Hameed is a dedicated and compassionate physiotherapist committed to helping patients recover mobility, reduce pain, and improve their quality of life through evidence-based physiotherapy techniques.

With extensive clinical experience, she specializes in musculoskeletal rehabilitation, posture correction, sports injury management, and women’s health physiotherapy.
